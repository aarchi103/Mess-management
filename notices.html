MESS RULES

1. Every student should come on time.

2. Do not waste food.

3. Place the chairs back at positions while leaving.

4. Each student should bring their mess card along with them.

5. Mess card should be in proper condition with details and photograph.

6. Take proper care of electronic appliances while using.

7. Any kind of celebration is not allowed.

8. Maintain proper discipline.

NOTICE

1. All the Ist year students belonging to New Mega Mess are instructed to take food in NMM only at proper time and not allowed to go and take food outside the mess.

2.This should be followed by all the Hostel Inmates so as to maintain mess discipline.

3.The Hostel Mess for PG and integrated students will remain closed for Summer Vacations.
